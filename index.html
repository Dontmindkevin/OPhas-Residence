Escape to Paradise

Nestled in the heart of Kajiado, Kenya, OPhas Residence offers an unparalleled escape from city life. Our exclusive hotel and resort provides the perfect weekend getaway, where luxury meets the authentic beauty of Maa land.

Immerse yourself in breathtaking landscapes, experience the rich culture of the Maasai community, and indulge in world-class hospitality. From sunrise views over the savannah to starlit evenings by the fire, every moment at OPhas Residence is crafted to create unforgettable memories.

Whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion, our resort offers the best that nature and luxury can provide in perfect harmony.
